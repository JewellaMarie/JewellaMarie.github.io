The ‘Hirap’ Chronicles: A Jewella-fied Memoir, Unfiltered

A facade of academic success and online fame lies a story of resilience forged in childhood trauma and teenage heartbreak.
Jewella’s journey is a raw and honest portrayal of navigating life’s challenges, from family turmoil to the pressures of college life. Discover how she transforms pain into strength, finding joy amidst the shadows of her past.

A story of love, loss, and the unwavering pursuit of self-discovery, this is a testament to the human spirit’s capacity to overcome adversity and find light in the darkest of times.

My not so 'CHILL'dhood Life


           1"Childhood is a wonderful time of life, but it can also be a difficult time of life. You have to learn to deal with all sorts of things." 2This quote from Fred Rogers resonated deeply with Jewella, whose childhood was far from idyllic. 3As the eldest child in a household marred by her parents' tumultuous relationship, she witnessed firsthand the pain of infidelity. 4The constant shouting and slamming doors created an unsettling environment, and the discovery of her father's infidelity at the age of six shattered her innocent view of the world. 5Add the fact that her school life wasn't pleasant either, be bullied by people within her years. 6She could also vividly remember her teacher throwing her meal away, and laughing for his own action, her classmate bullying her by throwing her books on the puddle and having to pick it up by herself. 7This was such a sad era for her.

          8This early trauma forced Jewella to mature beyond her years, grappling with emotions she shouldn't have had to confront at such a young age. 9While her parents' relationship remained tumultuous and her school life unpleasant, Jewella found solace in the love of her grandparents. 10 She cherished the simple joys of playing bingo with her grandmother and engaging in playful banter with her grandfather. 11She remembered pissing her lola off for always getting reward money whenever she wins and paying for her cards whenever she loses to her other playmates. 12Those wonderful times. She also remembers herself asking for money, five to ten pesos most of the time to her lolo for food or junkies but he gave her a thousand peso. 13She remembered regretting her decision of flaunting it to her grandmother because she confiscated the thousand peso bill and exchanged it for five pesos instead. 14I know what you’re thinking right now. 15“SAYANG!” and yup! Looking back, it was really sayang.

          16These moments of lightheartedness provided a much-needed escape from the turmoil at home. 17However, the sudden loss of her grandfather left a profound void in her life. 18Though her mother offered words of comfort, the grief was undeniable.

          19Unfortunately, Jewella's childhood was further complicated by a traumatic experience of sexual abuse at the hands of a family member, her cousin. 20Thankfully, she was strong enough to fight back and nothing more than touch happened. 21This deeply distressing event left lasting emotional scars. 22Yet, despite the hardships she endured, Jewella developed a resilience that allowed her to navigate these challenges and emerge stronger. 23This, an event she considered painful yet a wonderful lesson to never let anyone not worthy of your trust gain them.

          24Pushing aside the pessimism, thinking about the happy moments in her life, especially the love from her grandparents. 25She cherishes the memories of having all the toys she wanted, family car trips, and a childhood spent outdoors, unlike many kids today. 26Her mom is the best. She created a loving world for her sister and her. 27Despite limited resources, she made sure she had a wonderful childhood, filled with special experiences like extravagant meals, vacations, and parties. 28She even managed to send us them to a private school with a private bus.

          29She's an incredible mother, having to live by her belief that she’s gonna give the both of them a life she was unable to have during her time. 30She might've been an unlucky wife, but she is a successful mother to them. 31BEST MOM EVER!







AGE: PRIME-teen YEARS OLD


           1"Happy thirteenth, Jewella!" The air buzzed with joy as everyone celebrated Jewella's transition into her teenage years—her era, as some would say. 2This marked the beginning of a period of immense growth and learning, though not always bathed in happiness. 2Jewella blossomed, becoming stronger and wiser, navigating the complexities of adolescence. 3A significant turning point came with a school transfer, sparking a surge in academic excellence and a transformation from introverted to vivacious. 4She embraced leadership, consistently serving as class president, with flying colors during her high school graduation. 5This, however, is just a glimpse of the full story. 6A youthful romance led to heartbreak and betrayal. 7Naive and trusting, she gave her all, only to be abused, used, and cheated on— experiences no young girl should endure. 8While some might criticize her youthful indiscretions, it's undeniable that she was young and vulnerable, struggling to manage her emotions and capacity for love.

          9But from these ashes of pain rose an incredibly resilient young woman. 10She developed a sharp wit, unwavering bravery, and a carefree spirit. 11Despite having strict parents, she found ways to enjoy life with her friends—a secret she kept from her father. 12Her teenage years, after the initial heartbreak, were filled with joy and accomplishment. 13She excelled in various competitions, winning beauty pageants, singing contests, dance competitions, and poetry slams, gaining recognition and building lasting friendships. 14She nurtured self- love, self-care, and became more aware of the infidelities happening at home, even finding the courage to discuss it with her mother. 15She would often offer comforting words, saying (in Tagalog, of course), "Mom, it's okay if you let go of what pains you most. 16But I understand if your love for him is enough to still stay after everything. 17 I’m here." Yet, her teenage years weren't without their shadows.

          18Haunted by the fear of being bullied, she sometimes resorted to being mean to others as a defense mechanism. 19She erected walls around her heart, inadvertently causing pain to those sincere people around her. 20Her pursuit of excellence, fueled by high expectations, led to overthinking and eventually a diagnosis of anxiety. 22Education, once a sanctuary, became a battleground. 23The pandemic, however, became a catalyst for further growth. 24She became more emotionally detached, believing she could navigate life independently. 25Balancing online classes with live streaming, she gained a substantial following and earned a significant income, reaching up to 50,000 pesos a month. 26But the online world also brought its challenges. 27Insecurities arose due to negative comments and, more surprisingly, criticism from fellow female streamers. 28 Despite her resilience to men's inappropriate comments, the unexpected negativity from women, particularly given her advocacy for women's empowerment, was a blow.

          29Post-pandemic, Jewella continued to live with pride, cultivating strong and vibrant friendships, some faded with time, while others endured, strengthened by a commitment to stay connected and support each other, despite diverging paths. 30 They navigated the challenges of busy schedules, always finding time to be there for one another. 31At this point in her life, she envisioned a future of independence, living alone with her beloved dogs and showering her nieces with affection—the quintessential "single rich tita", as some might say. 32However, her path forward remained unclear. 33Choosing the seemingly safest option, she embarked on the challenging STEM strand in senior high school, which she successfully completed with flying colors with high honors. 34Through this academic journey, she discovered a passion for medicine and law, becoming torn between medtech, pharmaceutical sciences, and law. 35Ultimately, she found a field that encompassed all her interests: FORENSIC SCIENCE! 36Unfortunately, her mother, unwilling to let her go and concerned about the program's limited availability, discouraged her from pursuing it. 37Her mother's consolation was that it wasn't a high-paying career, something Jewella desired. 38And so, she found herself in college pursuing a degree in Information Technology.

          39In a nutshell, Jewella's teenage years were a rollercoaster of experiences, from the innocent joy of early adolescence to the pain of betrayal and the challenges of navigating social pressures and academic expectations. 40She experienced both immense heartbreak and incredible resilience, ultimately growing into a strong and independent young woman. 41The pandemic further solidified her self-reliance, though it also exposed the darker side of online fame. 42It was a roller coaster ride, but for the first time, she really enjoyed. 43SHE'S FINALLY GETTING THERE, A LITTLE MORE PUSH AND GROWTH!


UNIVERSIT-AINT FUN... YET


           1"HIRAP!" This became one of Jewella's signature phrases in college. 2Life, however, was smoother this time around. 3She was enjoying herself, actively participating in various events, serving as a first-year representative, and maintaining her status as a consistent university scholar. 4College life, though, had its stagnant moments. 5Initially grateful for her half-day schedule, she soon discovered it wasn't the norm. 6But those times were indeed golden. 7She excelled academically while enjoying her free time, playing billiards and socializing without financial worries, thanks to her past frugality. 8Yet, unwanted attention persisted. 9She still faced harassment from perverts, including an incident with a fellow organization member who forcibly touched her. 10Men from other colleges also made her uncomfortable with their unwanted advances. 11However, these experiences, while not new, were met with a newfound bravery. 12She navigated these situations gracefully and without force.

          13In November 2023, she met someone who finally piqued her interest, after a string of uninspiring encounters. 14He was a guy she met on the university's freedom wall, only to discover he attended the Caloocan campus, not Manila. 15Their initial interactions weren't really happy and fond, she hated him actually. 16But they grew closer with time, discovering mutual feelings. 17She was a freshman, and he was a senior, a semester behind in mechanical engineering, which allowed them to still cross paths on campus. 18Their relationship was a roller coaster of highs and lows, marked by traumas, but ultimately, she found peace and chose forgiveness. 19She had seen and experienced enough to understand.

          20From this point on, college became less exciting. 21Hectic schedules transformed her into a home-school girl, rarely socializing, spending her time in libraries or sleeping at home, interacting mainly with her partner and occasionally some friends. 22She maintained her high standards, driven by a fear of disappointing others, pushing herself to study even harder, sometimes to her detriment. 23She longed for a life free from the pressure to excel, yet she also didn't want to disappoint her parents, who were so happy with her achievements. 24Her mother, despite the ongoing infidelity, continued to forgive, a situation Jewella had long since detached herself from.

          25Life settled into a routine, secure and steady. 26She persevered through her college years, aiming for success. 27She also tutored her classmates sometimes for free lunch (kuripot padin huhu). 28But what really fulfills her is their act of being thankful for she had played a great role in their success to pass the college still until as of the moment. 29Whether or not she graduated with Latin honors in which it’s part of her plan, she knew she was trying her best in a course she didn't truly enjoy but chose for its perceived financial security. 30Pushing through a major she wasn't passionate about was difficult, but the desire for a comfortable future and desire to give her parents the best life, especially given that her mom crawled to let us finish in a private university, fueled her efforts.

          31Jewella's college years were marked by academic success, new friendships, and a developing romantic relationship. 32She faced continued harassment but met it with resilience. 33While college life became less stimulating, she remained focused on her studies, driven by a desire for financial stability, despite not enjoying her chosen field.
