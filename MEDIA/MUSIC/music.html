Why "I Love You"?

Genre: Ballad

"I Love You" by Billie Eilish is a hauntingly beautiful ballad that explores themes of love, heartbreak, and vulnerability. Its minimalist production and emotional lyrics make it a standout track. I liked this piece as this really strengthen my voice on the lower range and falsettos. Also, how it brings me the feeling of just laying in bed, rethinking all I have done and have not yet done even without the relation to the lyrics. This helps me release the heavy feeling in my heart during times I feel like I can't keep it anymore but I can't find a reason to cry. You know that feeling? The feeling of wanting to cry but can't cry anymore? That's basically currently my phase. I felt home in her voice. It might sound bias since I like Billie Eilish, but in terms of genre, I am mostly into ballad as these are the types of songs that I can follow and keep up to. This is basically my forte.

So yeah, if you're into R&B, especially the stuff with a bit of pop to it, we're definitely on the same wavelength.

Why "Sa Bawat Sandali"?

Genre: OPM Pop

"Sa Bawat Sandali" by Amiel Sol is a heartfelt Filipino song that captures the essence of love and longing. Its soulful melody resonates deeply with listeners. Now, let me speak in tagalog to feel every bits of my sentiment on this song. Basically, sobrang gaan ng loob ko kada nagpe-play 'tong kanta na'to. I feel at ease and safe and at the same time longing. As mentioned before, I had a grandma whom I dedicated all my passion to. The reason why I strive, the reason why I live, the reason why I want to keep on living. And if my lola was a song, this song is her. She is my safe space, my haven. Sobrang sakit sa pakiramdam sa mga panahong lugmok ako, pero nakaka-inspire sa mga panahong masaya. "Kapag magulo na ang mundo, ikaw ang payapang hinahanap ko, tumakbo ka rin patungo sa'kin, kapag bumibigat na ang iyong dibdib. Ika'y sasalubungin." she is that song. Also, my sister. Especially now that lola's gone, mommy is too occupied with her and her husband's affairs. I only got my sister now. Now, she is that song.

Anyways, enough of the drama. So basically, I just love, love, love pinoy pop music, alt rock pinoy, music, and most OPM songs and bands such as December Avenue, Silent Sanctuary, SpongeCola, Up Dharma Down, Amiel Sol, and Etc. I came to love filipino songs ever since I entered college cause I never knew I needed it as college is too bloody stressful!!!
