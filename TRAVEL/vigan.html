Welcome To Vigan


Why Vigan is Special to Me

As someone who loved history ever since reading a book about going back to the past and live in it, historical landscapes became one of my favorite kind of art. I was gonna put Binondo, Manila. But dream big, right? The feeling of being immersed in a story I've read during childhood keeps me eager of wanting to go to such place. This became one of the things that I imagine whenever I read that story. You should try reading it too, maybe you will also get to find the feeling of sanity and appreciation to such historical places. It is entitled, "I Love You Since 1892"

I cannot fully just say that it is only Vigan that makes my desire go wild but all the other historical places, but this is a place that's stuck in my head whenever I think of historical places



How to Get There

Vigan is accessible by bus or car from Manila, which is approximately 8-10 hours away. You can also fly to Laoag International Airport, which is about 2 hours from Vigan, and then take a bus or van to the city.



Other Places to Visit in Vigan

  • Calle Crisologo - A historic street lined with Spanish-era houses.
  • Bantay Bell Tower - A famous watchtower with stunning views of the city.
  • Syquia Mansion Museum - A glimpse into the life of a wealthy Filipino family during the Spanish era.
  • Vigan Cathedral - A beautiful church located in the heart of the city.
  • Pagburnayan Jar Factory - Learn the traditional art of pottery-making.
